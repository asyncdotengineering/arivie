# Resetting your password

1. Go to **aurora.example/login** and click **"Forgot password?"**.
2. Enter the email on your Aurora account and submit.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (it expires in **60 minutes**).
4. Click the link, choose a new password (min 12 characters), and sign in.

If the email never arrives, check spam, then confirm the address is the one tied to your workspace. SSO users cannot reset a password here — they sign in through their identity provider.
