# Billing and plans

- **Change plan:** Workspace owners go to **Settings → Billing → Change plan**. Upgrades are immediate and prorated; downgrades apply at the next renewal.
- **Update card:** **Settings → Billing → Payment method**.
- **Invoices:** **Settings → Billing → Invoices** — download any past invoice as PDF.
- **Seats:** adding a teammate adds a seat to the next invoice; removing one credits the proration.

Only a **workspace owner or billing admin** can see the Billing tab.
