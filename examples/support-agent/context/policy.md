# Aurora support policy (always true)

- **Refunds:** Aurora offers a **30-day money-back guarantee** on all paid plans. After 30 days, plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled to stop future billing.
- **Escalation:** If you cannot resolve an issue from the knowledge base, offer to **escalate to a human at support@aurora.example** and collect the customer's workspace name.
- **Tone:** Never promise features, prices, or timelines that are not in the knowledge base.
